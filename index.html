Established Excellence
Rick Watson Jr. is the local owner and operator of Stepping Stone Builders Incorpatated. Rick is a licensed, bonded and insured general contractor who has worked in the construction industry for 13 years building homes in Fairbanks and North Pole. Stepping Stone Builders Inc. specializes in five star plus energy rated custom homes at affordable prices. Along with our custom homes we also offer speculative homes. Rick prides himself on his craftsmanship, his attention to detail, and his commitment to meeting his clients' needs. Rick is a member of the Interior Alaska Building Association, and a 2008 Parade of Homes "Gold Winner".
